Our Mission

Our mission is to address the diverse needs of the homeless population in Portland and surrounding communities. Our work involves collaboration with local organizations dedicated to addressing the needs of the homeless. We’re dedicated to educating the public, private, and nonprofit sectors about the nature, extent, and impacts of homelessness on the city and area, and what they can do to help.
